Features

We have alot of unique features on our E-learning platform that will help you learn easier, work smoothly and acquire an exquisite educational experience. Here's some of the most significant features of our website.

Recommendation Engines

Is used to recommend content to students according to their specific preferences, interactions and interests. This will make students capable of reaching courses that they desire and find out about courses that they might need to enroll in.

Surveillance systems

Is used to monitor student’s behavior during tasks that needs supervision such as attempting exams, detecting plagiarism in students work, also such systems are used to supervise student posts and content for an appropriate text.

Auto Grading System

A sophisticated and automated grading system that's developed to allows instructors to quickly grade multiple complex computer literacy assignments. The Systems is used to evaluate the students' answers on quizzes/exams and assignments.(Either MCQ, tables or even Essay questions)

Our system offers many advantages over the other grading systems like: The OCR system and detecting the document structure. Grading complex structure questions like tables. Grading with respect to the degree of meaning similarity between documents using NLP and many other aspects, not just lexically like other systems.

Plagiarism Checker

Plagiarism is a mandatory feature in any E-Learning system, that's why we introduce our plagiarism detector which determines the degree of plagiarism between the student’s submissions (‘non’, ‘heavy’, ‘light’, ‘cut’).
Our plagiarism checker has the advantage of the high accuracy due to using high end model architecture and high investing in feature engineering.
